## 📝 COPY TONE GUIDELINES

### Keep This
- ✅ Conversational, not corporate jargon
- ✅ Honest, not hype or promises
- ✅ Personal, not anonymous or generic
- ✅ Action-oriented ("I do X, not Y")
- ✅ Humble, not arrogant ("I help," not "I'm the best")

### Add This
- ✨ **Fairness emphasis** (core differentiator)
- ✨ **Process emphasis** (not just personality)
- ✨ **ESCRO platform** (what's coming, why it matters)
- ✨ **Constitutional approach** (why you're different from competitors)
- ✨ **Accountability** (my name is on this, my reputation is currency)

### Examples of Good Copy

**❌ Bad:** "Revolutionary platform for B2B collaboration"  
**✅ Good:** "Fair process where both sides are protected, no matter who is more powerful"

**❌ Bad:** "Disrupt the matching industry"  
**✅ Good:** "I verify everyone, so you don't have to worry about credibility"

**❌ Bad:** "30+ happy clients"  
**✅ Good:** "Clients who came back 5+ times because fairness works"

**❌ Bad:** "Industry-leading technology"  
**✅ Good:** "Simple process, transparent arbitration, my personal guarantee"

**❌ Bad:** "Game-changing solution for freelancer risk"  
**✅ Good:** "Money protected until delivery. Both sides treated equally. Disputes resolved in 72 hours."
